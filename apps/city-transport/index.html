City Transport

Real-time GPS tracking for buses, trams, trolleybuses and minibuses across 70+ cities in 15 European countries.

Overview Support Privacy Policy

About

Know exactly where your transport is

City Transport shows the live position of buses, trams, trolleybuses, and minibuses on a map — across 70+ cities in 15 European countries, from Kyiv and Lviv to Rome, Warsaw, Berlin and Amsterdam.

Features

What's Inside

📍

Real-Time Tracking

See live vehicle positions on the map, updated at intervals you control — or refresh manually whenever you need.

🗺️

70+ Cities Across Europe

From Kyiv, Lviv and Kharkiv to Rome, Warsaw, Berlin and Amsterdam — live tracking in 15 European countries.

🕒

Arrival Times

Tap any stop to see which routes are approaching and how long until they arrive.

🎨

Three Interface Themes

Choose a theme that suits your preference — light, dark, or system default — for comfortable use at any time of day.

⚙️

Configurable Updates

Set your preferred refresh interval or switch to manual mode, and optionally share your location to center the map on you.

🧭

Route Finder

Plan a trip between two points and compare routes by travel time and number of transfers.
