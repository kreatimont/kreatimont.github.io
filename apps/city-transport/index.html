About

Know exactly where your bus is

City Transport shows the live position of buses, trams, trolleybuses, and minibuses on a map — so you can stop guessing and start planning your commute.

Features

What's Inside

📍

Real-Time Tracking

See live vehicle positions on the map, updated at intervals you control — or refresh manually whenever you need.

🗺️

28+ Ukrainian Cities

Covers major cities including Kyiv, Lviv, Kharkiv, and dozens more regional urban transit networks.

📅

Route Schedules

Browse route timetables so you can plan ahead even without a live connection.

🎨

Three Interface Themes

Choose a theme that suits your preference — light, dark, or system default — for comfortable use at any time of day.

⚙️

Configurable Updates

Set your preferred data refresh interval or switch to manual mode to control exactly when data updates.

📡

Optional Geolocation

Enable geolocation to center the map on your position, or use the app without sharing your location — your choice.
