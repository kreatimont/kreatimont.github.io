Privacy Policy

Applies to: Symbols — All Characters

Last updated: March 2025

Overview

Symbols — All Characters ("the App") is developed and maintained by Alexandr Nadtoka ("I", "me", "my"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

Symbols works entirely on-device and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No account or internet connection is required.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect:

  • Personal identifiable information
  • Analytics or usage data
  • Search queries or clipboard contents
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data

Local Data Storage

Favourites and recently used characters are stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted anywhere and is removed if you delete the App.

No Network Access

The App operates fully offline. The entire Unicode character database is bundled within the App — no network requests are made during normal use.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal data at risk. Locally stored preferences are protected by iOS device encryption.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal data from any user, including children. It is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
