About

Every character, at your fingertips

Whether you need a special symbol, an obscure Unicode character, or just want to know the HTML entity for an arrow — Symbols has it all. Search, browse, copy, and save any character from the full Unicode range directly on your device.

Features

What's Inside

Everything you need to find and use any Unicode character.

🔍

Powerful Search

Find any character by name, description, or Unicode code point. No more endless scrolling to find what you need.

📋

One-Tap Copy

Tap any character to instantly copy it to your clipboard — ready to paste anywhere on your device.

⭐

Favourites

Save your most-used characters to a Favourites list for instant access whenever you need them.

🕒

Recently Used

The App keeps track of recently accessed characters so you can quickly return to what you've used before.
