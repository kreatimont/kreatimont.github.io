Support

Need help with Symbols? Find answers below or reach out directly.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I copy a character?

Tap any character in the list to view its details, then tap the copy button to copy it to your clipboard. You can then paste it anywhere on your device.

How do I add a character to Favourites?

Open the character detail view by tapping a character, then tap the star or heart icon to add it to your Favourites list. Access your Favourites from the dedicated tab in the App.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. The entire Unicode database is bundled inside the App. It works fully offline — no internet connection needed.

How do I search for a character?

Use the search bar at the top of the character list. You can search by character name (e.g. "copyright"), category, or Unicode code point (e.g. "U+00A9").

Where can I find the HTML entity for a character?

Tap any character to open its detail view. The HTML entity code (e.g. &copy;) and Unicode code point are displayed there alongside the character itself.

My favourites disappeared after updating. What happened?

Favourites are stored locally on your device. In rare cases, a major app update may reset local storage. If this happens, please reach out via email and I'll do my best to help. For safety, iCloud backup (if enabled) may preserve this data between reinstalls.
