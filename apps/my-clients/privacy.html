Privacy Policy

Applies to: My Clients: assistant master

Last updated: March 2025

Overview

My Clients ("the App") is developed and maintained by Alexandr Nadtoka ("I", "me", "my"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

All data you enter into My Clients is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to any external server.

Information Stored by the App

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Client names, contact details, and notes you enter manually
  • Appointment dates, times, and reminders you create
  • Usage statistics calculated from your records

This data exists solely on your device. Deleting the App will permanently remove all stored data.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect or transmit:

  • Personal identifiable information to any server
  • Analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data

Notifications

The App may request permission to send local notifications for appointment reminders. These are generated entirely on-device and do not involve any network communication.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases (full access unlock, ad removal) processed through Apple's App Store. These transactions are handled entirely by Apple and are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. I do not receive or store any payment information.

Third-Party Advertising

The free version of the App may display ads. These ads may be served by Apple's advertising network (Apple Search Ads) or other frameworks included in the App. Please refer to Apple's privacy documentation for details on how ad-related data is handled.

Data Security

All client data you store in the App is protected by the security features built into iOS, including device encryption. I do not have access to any data stored in the App.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
