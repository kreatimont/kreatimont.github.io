FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new client?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen to open the new client form. Fill in the relevant details (name, contact info, notes) and save. Your client will appear in the list immediately.

How do I set an appointment reminder?

Open a client's profile, add an appointment with date and time, and enable the reminder toggle. The App will ask for notification permission on first use — make sure to allow it so reminders work correctly.

Is my data backed up?

Data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud backup enabled for your iPhone, app data will be included in your iCloud backup automatically. Deleting and reinstalling the App without a backup will erase your records.

What does the Full Access purchase include?

The Full Access in-app purchase unlocks all features of the App without any limitations. It is a one-time purchase — no subscription required.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to the App's settings and tap "Restore Purchases". Your previously purchased items will be restored as long as you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Does the App share my client data with anyone?

No. All client data is stored only on your device and is never sent to any external server. See the Privacy Policy for full details.
