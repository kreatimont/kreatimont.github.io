Features

Everything you need, nothing you don’t.

My Clients is built for independent professionals who want a clean, fast way to manage their work — no complexity, no monthly fees.

👤

Client Records

Store names, contacts, notes and full visit history for every client. Rebook in one tap, or call, message or open Instagram.

📅

Appointments & Reminders

Book sessions with service, price and time, and get automatic reminders from 30 minutes to a day before.

💰

Income & Expenses

Track income for any period, log expenses by category, and see your net profit. Prepayments and 28 currencies supported.

📊

Statistics & Reports

Clear income charts and trends, period comparisons and revenue goals. Export full reports to PDF and CSV.

🧩

Home-Screen Widgets

Today's appointments and this month's income at a glance — right on your home screen.

🌍

20 Languages & Themes

Light, dark or system theme, custom accent colors and 20 interface languages. Make it yours.
