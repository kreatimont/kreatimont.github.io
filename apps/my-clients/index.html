Features

Everything you need, nothing you don’t.

My Clients is built for independent professionals who want a clean, fast way to manage their work — no complexity, no monthly fees.

👤

Client Records

Store names, contact details, notes, and service history for every client. Everything in one place, always at your fingertips.

🔔

Appointment Reminders

Schedule upcoming sessions and get timely notifications. Never miss an appointment or forget to follow up.

📊

Monthly Statistics

See how your business is growing month over month — clients served, appointment trends, and more at a glance.

15 Languages

Available in 15 languages including English, Russian, Ukrainian, Spanish, French, German and more. Work in your language.

Fully Offline

All your data lives on your device. No account needed, no internet required, no cloud sync — your clients stay private.
