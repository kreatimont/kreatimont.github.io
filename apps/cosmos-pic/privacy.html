Privacy Policy

Applies to: Cosmos Pic

Last updated: March 2025

Overview

Cosmos Pic ("the App") is developed and maintained by Alexandr Nadtoka ("I", "me", "my"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

Cosmos Pic does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No account, sign-in, or personal information is required to use the App.

Data the App Accesses

The App fetches publicly available image and text data from NASA's Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD) API. This is a read-only public API — no personal data is sent to NASA as part of this request beyond what any standard HTTPS request includes (e.g. your IP address, handled by NASA's infrastructure according to NASA's Privacy Policy).

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personal identifiable information
  • Account or sign-in data
  • Location data
  • Analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Device identifiers

Saved Images

If you choose to save an image to your photo library, this action is performed locally on your device using iOS's standard photo-saving functionality. The App does not upload or store saved images anywhere.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal data at risk. All network requests are made over HTTPS.

Children's Privacy

Cosmos Pic does not collect personal data from any user, including children. The App is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
