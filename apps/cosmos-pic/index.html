About

The universe, on any day

NASA has been capturing and sharing stunning images of our universe every day since 1995 through their Astronomy Picture of the Day program. Cosmos Pic brings that entire archive to your fingertips — pick any date and see what was out there.

Curious what the cosmos looked like on your birthday? The day you met someone special? Any date back to June 16, 1995? Just tap and find out.

Features

Simple, beautiful, and endlessly explorable.
