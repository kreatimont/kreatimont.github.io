Teso: Couples

Daily shared prompts and reflections to help couples feel closer — one moment at a time.

Overview Support Privacy Policy

About

A private space for two

Modern relationships need space for honest conversation, emotional awareness, and intentional connection. Teso is a private space for couples to reflect, respond, and understand each other better — one day at a time.

Every day, you'll receive a shared prompt designed to spark meaningful thoughts, appreciation, or gentle self-reflection. You answer independently, and only when both of you respond do the answers open — creating a moment of mutual presence.

Teso is not about performance, tracking, or perfection. It's about slowing down, noticing each other, and building emotional intimacy naturally.

Features

How it works

Simple by design. Meaningful by nature.

💬

Daily Shared Prompts

Each day brings a new prompt crafted to spark honest thoughts, appreciation, or gentle self-reflection — for both of you.

🔒

Answer Independently

You each answer on your own. Answers reveal only after both partners respond — no pressure, no peeking.

🃏

Relationship Cards

Swipe cards and discover what you and your partner have in common. Matches appear only when both react the same way.

🗓️

Shared Events

Add dates, movie nights, and meaningful moments to your shared timeline — a private record of your life together.

🤝

AI Conflict Resolution

When things get hard, Teso offers a structured space to share perspectives and receive thoughtful, balanced analysis.

🔐

Fully Private

Your reflections stay between you and your partner. Teso is not a social network — it's a space just for two.
