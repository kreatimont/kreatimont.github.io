Privacy Policy

Applies to: Teso: Couples

Effective Date: March 2, 2026  ·  Developer: Alexandr Nadtoka

Teso: Couples ("App", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the mobile application Teso: Couples available on the Apple App Store.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use the App, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Account authentication information when signing in via:
    • Apple Sign In
    • Google Sign-In
  • Content you create within the App, such as:
    • Events (e.g., dates, movie nights, intimacy events)
    • Card swipes and preferences
    • Conflict resolution inputs and responses

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • Device type
  • iOS version
  • App version
  • Anonymous usage data
  • Diagnostic and crash data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable connection with your partner
  • Sync matched cards between partners
  • Provide AI-powered conflict resolution
  • Manage subscriptions and premium access
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Perform analytics and diagnostics
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Firebase

We use Firebase (by Google LLC) for authentication, analytics, crash reporting, and backend infrastructure.

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.2 RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions and purchases. RevenueCat may process subscription status, purchase history, and anonymous identifiers.

RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

3.3 Sign-In Providers

If you use Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In, those providers may process authentication data according to their respective privacy policies.

4. AI Conflict Resolution Feature

The App includes an AI-powered conflict resolution feature available to subscribed users.

When you and your partner submit opinions:

  • The submitted text is processed to generate analysis.
  • Data may be temporarily processed by secure AI service providers.
  • We do not use your private conflict data for advertising.
  • We do not sell this information.

We aim to minimize stored sensitive content and protect your privacy.

5. Subscriptions

Teso: Couples offers optional in-app subscriptions that unlock conflict resolution, additional card packs, and premium features. All payments are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not directly store or process your payment information.

6. Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information:

  • As long as your account is active
  • As necessary to provide services
  • As required to comply with legal obligations

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see Section 10).

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including access to your data, correction of inaccurate data, deletion of your data, and withdrawal of consent.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: nadtoka.o.work@gmail.com

10. Account Deletion

You may request account deletion by using in-app deletion (if available) or contacting us via email. Upon deletion, your personal information will be removed from active systems within a reasonable timeframe, except where legally required to retain it.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries outside your country of residence. By using the App, you consent to such transfers where applicable.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Effective Date." Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
