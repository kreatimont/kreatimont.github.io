FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play the Flag Quiz?

Open Mapora and tap "Guess the Flag" on the home screen. You'll be shown a flag and four country name options — tap the correct one. Your score is tracked locally on your device.

What is Panorama Finder?

Panorama Finder uses Apple's Look Around feature to drop you into a random street-level view somewhere in the world. Your challenge is to figure out which country you're in. It's like a geography guessing game powered by real imagery.

Does Mapora work offline?

The Flag Quiz and Atlas work fully offline. The Panorama Finder requires an internet connection to load street imagery from Apple Maps.

Does Mapora collect any personal data?

No. Mapora does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your progress and scores are stored locally on your device. See the Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I reset my quiz progress?

You can reset your progress from within the app in Settings. If you delete and reinstall the app, all local data will be cleared.

I found a bug or incorrect data. How do I report it?

Please email me at nadtoka.alexandr@gmail.com with details about what you found. I appreciate every report — it helps make the app better for everyone.
