Privacy Policy

Applies to: Mapora

Last updated: March 2025

Overview

Mapora ("the App") is developed and maintained by Alexandr Nadtoka ("I", "me", "my"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

Your privacy is important to me. In short: Mapora does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Information Collected

Mapora does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identifiable information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports sent to third-party services

All app data (quiz scores, progress, settings) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

Mapora uses Apple's Look Around feature, which is part of Apple Maps. This feature is provided directly by Apple and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. I do not control or have access to any data Apple may process as part of this service.

No other third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, or advertising networks are integrated in the App.

Children's Privacy

Mapora is suitable for all ages. Because the App does not collect any personal data, it complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal data at risk. All data stored on-device is protected by the security measures built into iOS.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
