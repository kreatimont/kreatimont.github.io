Privacy Policy

Applies to: Mapora

Effective Date: June 4, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how the Mapora app ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information.

1. No Data Collection

Mapora does not collect, store, track, or share any personal data or usage information from users.

  • We do not collect your name, email, location, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not use cookies or analytics tools.
  • We do not integrate with third-party tracking or advertising services.

2. Offline and Local Functionality

Mapora works primarily on your device. Features like flag quizzes and atlas exploration function without requiring internet access or user data.

3. Panorama Feature

The "Find Your Location by Panorama" feature uses Apple's MapKit Look Around to provide 360° panoramic views. These panoramas are streamed directly from Apple and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
Mapora does not access, collect, or store any location data from this feature.

4. Children's Privacy

Since Mapora does not collect any data, it is safe for use by users of all ages, including children under 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. If we do, we will notify users by updating the policy within the app and changing the effective date at the top.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us at:
